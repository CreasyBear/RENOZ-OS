# Product Context

Renoz v3 serves operations teams that manage customers, orders, pipeline, warranties, and reporting. Reporting must be reliable, standardized, and tied to the data layer for multi-tenant environments.

## Problems Solved
- Fragmented reporting and inconsistent domain ownership.
- Lack of standardized flows between schema, validation, server functions, and hooks.

## User Experience Goals
- Consistent, trustworthy reporting data.
- Predictable UI and filtering behaviors across settings and reports.
- Low-friction management of scheduled reports and KPI targets.
